Student Bio

Tomek Regulski

Tomek Regulski is a musician based in NYC. As a coomposer, he has written several works for small and large ensembles. He also has been studying Hindustani music for 15 years, and plays both the sitar and surbahar. Additionally, Tomek is active in the Natural Food and Beverage Industry as a marketing specialist. When he is not working or practicing, he finds great joy in the kitchen.
